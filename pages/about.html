About Canteen

CANTEEN MADE SINCE 12/OCTOBER/2010

Canteens originated as military containers for carrying water but evolved into dining facilities in workplaces and schools during the 19th century. They provide affordable meals and promote social interaction in various settings, including factories, educational institutions, and hospitals.

Breakfast

Breakfast in the canteen often features a variety of options, from hearty scrambled eggs and crispy bacon to fresh fruit and yogurt.

Lunch

Lunch in the canteen typically offers a diverse menu, including sandwiches, salads, and warm entrees like pasta or stir-fry.

Dinner

Dinner in the canteen usually features a more relaxed atmosphere, with options like grilled meats, vegetarian dishes, and comforting sides such as mashed potatoes or roasted vegetables.
